# Privacy Policy - YBS Guide

Last updated: 2026-06-03

YBS Guide does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Location: The app requests device location permission to show nearby bus stops.
Your location is processed on-device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Data: All route data is stored locally on your device.
The app periodically checks for route data updates from a public GitHub repository.
No personal information is included in these requests.

Analytics: This app does not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.
