YBS Guide is the complete offline guide for Yangon Bus Service (YBS) routes.

Find the right bus in seconds — search by route number, stop name, or township in both English and Burmese (မြန်မာဘာသာ).

✅ 139 YBS routes with bilingual stop names
🗺 Interactive Yangon map with bus stop markers
📍 GPS nearby stops — find buses close to you
🔍 Search in English or Burmese script
⭐ Save favorite routes for quick access
🏠 Set Home and Work shortcuts
🚌 Route schedules and departure times
📵 Full offline support — no internet needed
🔄 Route data updates automatically

Perfect for:
- Daily commuters in Yangon
- New residents learning the bus network
- Visitors navigating the city by bus

All 139 YBS routes · All major townships covered:
Insein, Hlaing, Thaketa, Tharrmwe, North Okkalapa, South Okkalapa, Thingangyun, Dagon Seikkan, Shwepyithar, Mingaladon, Hmawbi, and more.

Data is maintained and updated via our open data project. Route updates reach your app automatically — no reinstall needed.
